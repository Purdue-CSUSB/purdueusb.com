What is Kanban?

The Kanban Challenge is a fun, team-based event, where members solve tasks and work together to beat the clock to win big.

Challenges vary from serious to silly (logic puzzles to wrapping someone up in toilet paper) , and no Computer Science background necessary! We will put you into teams of four, so don't worry about not having a team.

To learn more about Kanban, click here. It's used by companies everywhere for teams of all sizes!
